Our Profile

At Umbrella, we pride ourselves on pushing the boundaries of pharmaceutical innovation and ethical ambiguity. From cough syrups to top-secret viral weaponry (oops, did we say that out loud?), we’re committed to making the world a healthier place... one highly classified experiment at a time. Our scientists work around the clock in underground facilities (totally normal) to bring you products that might extend life, cure disease, and occasionally unleash mild zombie outbreaks. Mistakes were made, sure — but name one great company that didn’t accidentally create a bio-organic weapon or two.

Our Mission

“To redefine wellness through science, secrecy, and a strong PR department.”

Our Values

  • 💉 Innovation (but like, the sketchy kind)
  • 🔬 Research (on willing participants… mostly)
  • 🧟‍♂️ Resilience (especially in the face of outbreaks of our own making)
  • 😇 Responsibility (our legal team insisted we include this)

Whether you're looking for cutting-edge therapeutics or just a vitamin that won’t mutate your DNA (no promises), Umbrella Pharmaceuticals is here for you—until everything goes horribly wrong.
